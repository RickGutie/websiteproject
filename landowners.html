Services For Landowners:

Asset Diversification:

We work with our Landowners to locate and establish "Habitat Banks." This allows our Landowners to leverage unused and poor quality land assets to generate BNG Units. The subsequent sale of these units helps to diversify their income streams and opens up lucrative natural capital opportunities.

Natural Capital Asset Management:

Alongside our diversification solutions, we also take a holistic approach to Natural Capital.

We utilise our expertise to target layering opportunities within the habitat; such as Carbon Credits from planting trees. Where any opportunites are available a full report will be produced and provided to the Landowner ensuring complete transparency.

Asset Sourcing:

For our clients who have established "Habitat Banks" with us before, we provide sourcing solutions for further future projects.

We utilise our industry expertise to source, evaluate and reccomend further sites for "Habitat Bank" creation and work in partnership with our Landowners to maximise the return on investment from aquisition to disposal and continued management of the site.
